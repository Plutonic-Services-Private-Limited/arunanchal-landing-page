APST Stipend Programme

1. Aids schedule tribe students in Arunachal Pradesh.
2. Satisfactory progress, regular attendance, and good conduct.
3. Stipends not issued for misconduct, strikes, irregular attendance,

Post Matric Scholarship Programme

1. Centrally sponsored scheme with 100% central assistance.
2. Income limit: Rs. 2.50 lakh per annum.
3. Covers recognized post-matric courses; excludes training courses.
